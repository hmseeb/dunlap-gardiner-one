Our Team

Meet the Attorneys of
Dunlap Gardiner, LLP

Our team brings together skilled attorneys focused on delivering thoughtful, effective legal counsel across a wide range of practice areas. We are committed to affordable, personalized representation for every client we serve.

Founding Partners

Wesley C. Dunlap

Founding Partner

Wesley C. Dunlap is a founding partner of Dunlap Gardiner, LLP. A graduate of Georgia State University College of Law, Wes has built his practice around civil litigation and business law, bringing meticulous preparation and strategic thinking to every matter he handles.

Wes is deeply committed to providing affordable, personalized representation to both individuals and businesses throughout the metro Atlanta area. He understands that hiring an attorney can be a stressful experience, and he works to make the legal process as clear and painless as possible for every client.

Civil Litigation Business Law Georgia State University College of Law

Brian M. Gardiner

Founding Partner

Brian M. Gardiner co-founded Dunlap Gardiner, LLP in 2009 alongside his colleague and fellow Georgia State University College of Law alumnus, Wesley Dunlap. Brian's practice centers on creditor rights, collections, and HOA law, areas in which he has developed deep expertise over more than fifteen years of practice.

Brian represents homeowner associations in drafting covenants and bylaws, handling disputes, and advising on governance matters. He also counsels finance companies and banks on a wide range of legal matters, providing reliable and results-driven representation.

Associate Attorneys

Evan Barnard

Senior Associate

A licensed Georgia attorney, Evan Barnard is a senior member of our litigation team focusing on civil collections, creditor representation, and general civil litigation matters across the metro Atlanta area.

Working closely with the firm's founding partners, Evan brings diligent case management and client-focused advocacy to every engagement, helping businesses and financial institutions protect their interests efficiently and effectively.

Civil Litigation Collections Creditor Representation

Joel Glaze

Associate Attorney

Joel Glaze supports the firm's homeowner association and community law practice, assisting clients with covenant drafting, bylaw preparation, and the resolution of disputes between associations and homeowners.

With a strong foundation in Georgia real property and community association law, Joel helps HOA clients navigate governance challenges, enforcement actions, and collections matters with practical, cost-effective solutions.

HOA Law Community Associations Real Property

Sam Jordan

Associate Attorney

Sam Jordan handles business law and banking matters for the firm, providing counsel to finance companies, banks, and small businesses on transactional issues, contract review, and regulatory compliance throughout Georgia.

Dedicated to the firm's mission of affordable and accessible legal services, Sam works directly with clients to understand their business goals and deliver straightforward, actionable legal advice that supports long-term success.
