Our Story

Founded on Friendship
and Shared Purpose

Dunlap Gardiner, LLP was established by two friends from Georgia State University College of Law — Wesley C. Dunlap and Brian M. Gardiner. The attorneys opened the firm with the intent to help businesses and individuals throughout the metro Atlanta area with a wide range of legal issues and problems.

The firm was formed in 2009 in Douglasville, Georgia. Since that time, Dunlap Gardiner has grown to 5 attorneys and a support staff of twenty. In 2014, the firm moved from Douglasville to Hiram in order to accommodate the company's growth. The firm also opened a new office in McDonough, Georgia, further expanding its reach throughout the region.

Since the firm's inception, Dunlap Gardiner, LLP has focused on a wide range of general civil litigation matters. We handle collection matters and creditor bankruptcy issues. We also handle homeowner association matters, including drafting of covenants and bylaws and representing associations regarding any issues that may arise. The firm also represents many finance companies and banks and handles a wide range of legal matters for these clients.

Wes and Brian have dedicated themselves to providing affordable representation while also maintaining close relationships with each of their clients. The attorneys understand that hiring an attorney for any reason can be a trying time for anyone, and they take this into consideration when dealing with their clients. Wes and Brian strive to make the legal process, whether adversarial or not, as painless as possible.
