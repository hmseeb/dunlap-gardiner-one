About Our Firm

Two Friends.
One Shared Mission.

Dunlap Gardiner, LLP was established by two friends from Georgia State University College of Law — Wesley C. Dunlap and Brian M. Gardiner. Founded in 2009 in Douglasville, Georgia, the firm was built with the intent to help businesses and individuals throughout the metro Atlanta area with a wide range of legal issues.

Since its inception, the firm has grown to five attorneys and a support staff of twenty. In 2014, the firm relocated to Hiram to accommodate its growth, and later expanded with a second office in McDonough, Georgia.
